THE NEURAL STUDIO · 05 · THE MOTION VOCABULARY

Four ways a mind can move.

The same handful of neurons, animated four ways, so the motion is a decision you feel. A, B and C are the shipping SVG language: accessible, cheap, reduced-motion honest. D adds a canvas "living tissue" layer: more alive, but decorative-only, heavier, and invisible to a screen reader.

A · DENDRITES GROW · THE DRAW-IN
the mind drawing itself in: threads sweep, marks land. one-shot on load.
B · SYNAPSE FIRES · A THOUGHT CONNECTING · HOVER
hover a neuron: a bright pulse runs the connection. the payoff gesture.
C · IDLE DRIFT · A MIND AT REST
a slow sway, and every few seconds one connection fires on its own. never still, never busy.
D · SVG + CANVAS TISSUE · THE RICHNESS CEILING
the neurons ride a living particle tissue. COST: DECORATIVE ONLY · NO A11Y · MORE GPU

REDUCED MOTION → A/B/C/D all render the static, grown, connected still. no draw-in, no drift, no pulse, no canvas loop.
